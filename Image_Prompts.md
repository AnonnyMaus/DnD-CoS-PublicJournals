# Curse of Strahd - Session Image Prompts

Here are 14 Midjourney/DALL-E style prompts designed to capture the exact dark fantasy tone of each session:

## Session 1: Death House
> **Prompt:** A dark fantasy landscape showing a gloomy, brooding, tall Victorian townhouse surrounded by dense, oppressive magical fog. Gothic horror style, muted colors, Dungeons and Dragons, Curse of Strahd tone, dark and eerie, highly detailed.

## Session 2: The Burial
> **Prompt:** A somber, dark fantasy graveyard in a gloomy medieval village. A small group of adventurers burying a wooden coffin while thick mist creeps along the tombstones. Gothic horror, muted colors, Dungeons and Dragons, Curse of Strahd tone, moody lighting.

## Session 3: Escape to Vallaki 
> **Prompt:** A glowing, mystical Vistani encampment in a dark forest at night. Colorful wagons circling a large campfire, with one prominent fortune teller's tent. Dark fantasy, gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style, mysterious and highly detailed.

## Session 4: A Bone Mystery
> **Prompt:** The interior of a creepy, dusty medieval coffin maker's shop. Stacks of wooden caskets illuminated by dim candlelight, thick cobwebs, feeling of impending danger. Dark fantasy, gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style, hyperrealistic.

## Session 5: All Coffins Brake Loose
> **Prompt:** A desperate sprint through narrow, cobblestone medieval streets at night. Dark, shadowy vampire spawn giving chase in the background under the moonlight. Action-packed, dark fantasy, gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style.

## Session 6: The Hangover
> **Prompt:** A dark fantasy vineyard under siege. A dilapidated stone winery building surrounded by twisted, thorny vines and dozens of small, woody needle blights creeping out of the mist. Gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style, eerie green and brown tones.

## Session 7: Yester Hill Battle for the Eternal Gemstone
> **Prompt:** A massive, terrifying ritual atop a stormy hill. Evil druids dancing around a colossal, animated dead tree monster (Tree Blight) with lightning striking in the background. Epic fantasy combat, gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style, dynamic lighting.

## Session 8: Vallaki Trouble Brewing
> **Prompt:** A chaotic, bloody jailbreak in a dark medieval dungeon. Heavily armored guards clashing with adventurers in torchlit stone corridors. Gritty, dark fantasy, Dungeons and Dragons style, intense action, high contrast lighting.

## Session 9: Thorns & Choices for Vallaki
> **Prompt:** A dark fantasy town square with buildings engulfed in roaring flames at night. Adventurers desperately working to save civilians silhouetted against the bright orange fire. Dramatic, chaotic, gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style.

## Session 10: Judgement Day
> **Prompt:** The interior of a grand but worn stone church. A fierce duel between an armored paladin and a ghostly, fiery knight (Warden of Flame) while townsfolk watch in the shadows. Epic fantasy, Dungeons and Dragons style, glowing radiant and fire magic.

## Session 11: Ousted out of Vallaki
> **Prompt:** A massive, grotesque toad-like demon (Hezrou) tearing its way into a dimly lit aristocratic manor room through a magical glowing glyph. Panic and destruction, dark fantasy, Dungeons and Dragons style, horrific and imposing.

## Session 12: The Pilgrimage to Krezk
> **Prompt:** A freezing, misty river in a dark forest. Adventurers struggling to pull a heavy, dripping wooden coffin out of the icy water using fishing nets. Gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style, cold blue and gray color palette.

## Session 13: A Creepy Bride
> **Prompt:** Inside a macabre, candlelit abbey. A deranged priest standing proudly next to a horrifying, beautifully dressed flesh golem bride stitched together from body parts. Extremely creepy, dark fantasy, gothic horror, Dungeons and Dragons style.

## Session 14: A Weary Fight
> **Prompt:** A brutal, bloody fighting pit deep inside a dark cavern. Exhausted adventurers fiercely battling a pack of massive, snarling werewolves. Epic fantasy combat, gritty, visceral, Dungeons and Dragons style, dynamic and terrifying.
